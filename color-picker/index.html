Color Picker & Converter

The Snappet Color Picker is a free online tool that converts colors between HEX, RGB, and HSL with a live preview and a WCAG contrast checker.

Features

  • Convert HEX ⇄ RGB ⇄ HSL
  • Live color preview
  • WCAG contrast ratio checker
  • One-click copy of any format

Frequently asked questions

Can it check color contrast for accessibility?

Yes. It shows the WCAG contrast ratio between two colors and whether it passes AA/AAA.
